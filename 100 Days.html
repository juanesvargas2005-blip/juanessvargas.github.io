DATA:

Almost 25% of the Ecuadorian population lacks access to basic health services (Pan American Health Organisation).

PROBLEM:

Isolation in rural areas makes it difficult for mothers to access proper care for their babies, putting their future at risk.

SOLUTION:

Connect mothers from all around to guide them in the complex process of the first 1000 days of maternity.

We create an app to guide mothers in the most critical window of development: the first 1000 days of life. Thanks to satellite connectivity, it works everywhere.
