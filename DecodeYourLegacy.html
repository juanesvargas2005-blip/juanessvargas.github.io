DATA:

More than 85% of young people express their identity through style, and for more than 80%, clothing is deeply connected to their culture.

PROBLEM:

Second-generation migrants often feel disconnected from their roots, as they grow up adopting local trends without truly understanding the cultural value behind their traditional clothing.

INSIGHT:

I feel like I don't belong anywhere.

SOLUTION:

Help second-gen migrants reconnect with their heritage through the stories behind their traditional clothing.


Depop launches Decode Your Legacy: a space in the app where users upload photos of family garments to uncover their cultural origins, meanings, and history—turning fashion into a journey of identity.
